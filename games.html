Emerald Isle

May 2024 - Present

Emerald Isle is a game that takes place in a small coastal town on the east coast of the united states. After being shipwrecked by Hurricane Hazel, you awake in the town of Emerald Isle. A seemingly normal small town ravaged by the hurricane. You must help rebuild the town, but things are more mystical than first meets the eye. Challenge bosses that threatened your new home, Build relationships with a fun and diverse cast of characters, gather resources to build, and become a great fisherman
