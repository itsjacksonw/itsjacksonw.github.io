Ghost Cafe

October 2022 - November 2022

Play as a ghost waiter serving food to hungry ghosts. You can hold one food item at a time in each hand--stand near food to grab it, and stand near a ghost to serve it. You can also throw used plates in the trash cans to make room for more customers. At the end of the night, you'll be able to see how much you scored. Try to get a high score by delivering orders in a timely manner to your spooky guests! Created by a group of 3 programmers and 3 artists.

Responsibilities:

- Player Mechanics
- Game Manager
- DevOps

Tools:

- Unity
